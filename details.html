FASTEST LINE FOLLOWER

Pavan R Gond     Vishwas Bhat

9448827825        7795012652


Robotics event where teams race autonomous bots along a black line on a white track, with penalties for deviations

Type:Team (2-4 members)
Duration: 7 hours (9 minutes per match (2 minutes setup, 2 minutes technical timeout, 2 minutes buffer, 3 minutes race))
Rules:
  1. Robots must be fully autonomous with no manual intervention during the race.
  2. The robot must follow a black line on a white track.
  3. Deviating from the track incurs a 5-second penalty per incident.
  4. A bot must complete the track within the 3-minute time limit to avoid disqualification.
  5. Manual interference results in disqualification unless authorized by the referee.
Rounds:
  1. Round 1 :Knockout Round – Bots race on a simple track; fastest bots qualify for the final round.
  2. Round 2:Final Judgment Round – Qualified bots race on a more complex track; the bot with the shortest time wins.
Judgment Criteria:
  1. Time taken to complete the track.
  2. Accuracy in following the track.
  3. Smoothness of the robot’s trajectory.
  4. Penalties for deviating or exceeding time.

ROBO SOCCER

Pavan R Gond     Vishwas Bhat

9448827825        7795012652


Knockout tournament where teams control robots to score goals within a timed match.

Type:Team (2-4 members)
Duration: 7 hours (5 minutes per match (2-minute rounds, 1-minute technical timeout in between))
Rules:
  1. Knockout format with teams advancing based on wins.
  2. Matches are divided into two 2-minute rounds, with a 1-minute technical timeout.
  3. Teams may request one additional 1-minute timeout without penalty; extended timeouts incur a 1-point deduction per extra minute (up to 3 minutes).
  4. A goal is worth 1 point, and the ball remains in play after scoring.
  5. Robots must be within 30cm x 30cm x 30cm dimensions and not exceed 5.5kg.
  6. Robots can push or hit the ball but cannot grab or trap it.
  7. No deliberate interference or damage to opponents’ bots; violations lead to disqualification.
Rounds:
  1. Round 1 :Knockout match (5-minute duration, divided into two 2-minute rounds).
  2. Round 2: Continued knockout rounds with similar match duration until the final.
Judgment Criteria:
  1. Number of goals scored during the match.
  2. Successful robot interaction with the ball.
  3. Adherence to fair play and technical rules.
  4. Deductions for penalty violations (e.g., extended timeouts, deliberate damage).

Electronics circuit-based hands-on event where teams answer the given questions and prepare the circuit by solving the errors in the existing circuit diagram.
