Payment Mode

Offline Mode

After selecting this mode, you will receive a QR code via email. Present this QR code at the event venue for scanning to confirm your registration. Please note that the payment status will remain pending until you pay on-site at the event.

Online Mode

After choosing this mode, scan the provided QR code using your UPI app and pay the required amount. Once the payment is complete, you’ll receive a QR code via email. This QR code must be scanned at the event venue to confirm your registration.

Additionally, you’ll need to upload a snapshot of the payment receipt from your UPI app, which includes the transaction ID, as proof for verification.
